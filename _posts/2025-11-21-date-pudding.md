## Date Pudding

### Ingredients

**Pudding**

- 1 c. chopped dates  
- 1 tsp. soda  
- 1 c. hot water  
- 1 egg  
- 1 c. sugar  
- 2 Tbsp. butter  
- 1 1/2 c. flour  
- 1 tsp. baking powder  
- 1/2 c. chopped walnuts  

**Topping**

- 1/2 c. sugar  
- 1/2 c. water  
- 1/2 c. chopped dates  
- 1/2 c. chopped nuts  
- 1 Tbsp. butter  

---

### Directions

1. Mix **1 cup chopped dates** with **soda** and **hot water**; let stand.  
2. In a separate bowl, combine **egg, sugar, butter, flour, baking powder, and walnuts**.  
3. Stir this mixture into the first mixture.  
4. Pour into a **greased 8- or 9-inch square pan**.  
5. Bake **35 minutes** in a **350°F oven**.  
6. While the cake bakes, make the topping by **cooking all topping ingredients together until thick**.  
7. When the cake is removed from the oven, **pour the hot topping over the warm cake**.  
8. Serve with **whipped cream**.
