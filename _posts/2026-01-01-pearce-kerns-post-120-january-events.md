## Overview {#overview}

This **Steak, Potato, Egg & Cheese Breakfast Casserole** is a hearty, make-ahead dish perfect for weekends, holidays, or feeding a crowd. Thinly sliced steak, tender potatoes, sautéed onions, eggs, and melted cheese come together in a classic, filling breakfast bake.

---

## Ingredients {#ingredients}

### Main Ingredients {#main-ingredients}

- 1 to 1½ lb **thinly sliced steak**  
  *(sirloin, shaved steak, ribeye, or flank)*
- 2 lb **potatoes**, thinly sliced  
  *(Yukon Gold or russet)*
- 1 large **onion**, thinly sliced
- 8–10 large **eggs**
- 1½ cups **milk** or **half-and-half**
- 2 cups **shredded cheese**  
  *(cheddar, Colby-Jack, Monterey Jack, or provolone)*

### Seasonings & Cooking {#seasonings}

- 2 Tbsp **olive oil** or **butter**
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 1 tsp **paprika** *(smoked preferred)*
- ½ tsp **dried thyme or rosemary** *(optional)*
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced *(optional)*

---

## Instructions {#instructions}

### Step 1 – Prepare the Oven {#step-1}

Preheat oven to **375°F (190°C)**.  
Lightly grease a **9×13-inch baking dish**.

---

### Step 2 – Par-Cook the Potatoes {#step-2}

1. Heat 1 Tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  
2. Add sliced potatoes, season with salt and pepper.  
3. Cook for **8–10 minutes**, stirring occasionally, until just tender.  
4. Remove from skillet and set aside.

---

### Step 3 – Cook Onions and Steak {#step-3}

1. Add remaining oil to the skillet.  
2. Sauté onions (and garlic, if using) until soft and lightly golden.  
3. Add steak, paprika, herbs, salt, and pepper.  
4. Cook quickly until steak is just no longer pink.  
   *Do not overcook.*

---

### Step 4 – Assemble the Casserole {#step-4}

1. Layer potatoes evenly in the baking dish.  
2. Spread steak and onion mixture on top.  
3. Sprinkle with **1½ cups of cheese**.

---

### Step 5 – Add Eggs {#step-5}

1. Whisk eggs with milk, a pinch of salt, and pepper.  
2. Pour evenly over the casserole.  
3. Top with remaining cheese.

---

### Step 6 – Bake {#step-6}

- Cover loosely with foil and bake **30 minutes**.  
- Remove foil and bake an additional **15–20 minutes**, until the center is set and the top is lightly golden.

---

### Step 7 – Rest and Serve {#step-7}

Let stand for **10 minutes** before slicing and serving.

---

## Make-Ahead & Variations {#variations}

### Make-Ahead {#make-ahead}

- Assemble the casserole the night before.
- Cover and refrigerate.
- Bake in the morning, adding **10 extra minutes** if cold.

### Variations {#recipe-variations}

- **Steakhouse Style:** Add mushrooms and provolone.
- **Southwestern:** Pepper jack cheese + green chiles.
- **Extra Crispy Top:** Broil for 2–3 minutes after baking.
- **Lower Carb:** Substitute potatoes with turnips or cauliflower.

---

## Serving Suggestions {#serving-suggestions}

Serve with fresh fruit, toast, or a light green salad for balance.
