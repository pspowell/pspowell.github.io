# Turkish Doner Kebab {#turkish-doner-kebab}

## Meat Ingredients {#meat-ingredients}
- 2 lb ground beef
- 1 medium yellow onion, grated & squeezed dry
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- **3 tsp** sweet paprika
- **2 tsp each:** garlic powder, onion powder, sumac, cumin, oregano
- 1 tsp mustard seed
- 1 tsp coriander
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Sauce Ingredients {#sauce-ingredients}
- 1/2 cup mayo
- 2 heaping tbsp labneh
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp za’atar
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt to taste

## Roasted Veg {#roasted-veg}
- 3 red bell peppers, sliced
- 3 jalapeños, sliced
- 2 onions, sliced
- Olive oil + salt

## Directions (Meat) {#directions-meat}
1. Combine ground beef, grated onion, garlic, and all spices. Mix until sticky and uniform.
2. Place on parchment, cover with another sheet, roll into a tight log removing air pockets.
3. Chill for one hour.
4. Bake at **400°F** for **30 minutes**. Remove parchment. Optionally crisp at **425°F** for 5 minutes.
5. Rest 10 minutes, then slice thin.

## Directions (Sauce) {#directions-sauce}
1. Blend all sauce ingredients until smooth and creamy.
2. Chill.

## Directions (Veg) {#directions-veg}
1. Spread peppers, jalapeños, and onions on a sheet pan.
2. Drizzle with olive oil + salt.
3. Roast at **425°F** until charred and soft.

## Assemble {#assemble}
- Toast pitas  
- Spread sauce  
- Add sliced doner meat  
- Top with roasted veggies  
- Finish with extra sauce  

Enjoy!
