## Buying & Verification Checklist {#verification}

Before committing to freezer beef:

- Ask **which processor** will be used
- Confirm **live weight vs hanging weight vs take-home weight**
- Clarify **cut sheet options**
- Ask whether pricing includes:
  - Processing
  - Cut & wrap
  - Disposal fees
- Confirm **pickup timing** and storage requirements
