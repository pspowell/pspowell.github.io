# Dano’s Dill-Stacker Bloody Mary

A bold, tangy Bloody Mary built around dill stackers and Dano’s seasoning, from the warped minds of Tony Markin and Jim Baker.

---

## Ingredients (1 drink)

- 2 oz vodka  
- 4–6 oz Bloody Mary mix (Zing Zang, Mr & Mrs T, or homemade)  
- 1–2 tsp Dano’s Original or Dano’s Spicy  
- 1–2 dill stacker pickle slices (plus a splash of the juice)  
- 1–2 dashes Worcestershire sauce  
- 1 dash hot sauce (optional)  
- Black pepper (optional)  
- Ice  

---

## Garnish Ideas

- Dill stacker spear  
- Celery stalk  
- Pickled green bean  
- Lime wedge  
- Bacon strip  
- Cheese cube + olive skewer  

---

## Instructions

1. Fill a tall glass with ice.  
2. Add vodka.  
3. Add Bloody Mary mix.  
4. Add Dano’s seasoning and stir well to dissolve.  
5. Add a splash of dill-pickle juice (key for the tang).  
6. Add Worcestershire and hot sauce to taste.  
7. Stir, taste, and adjust seasoning.  
8. Garnish with dill stackers and your preferred toppings.

---

## Optional “Chef’s Kiss” Version

Shake the drink (without ice) in a shaker to blend in the Dano’s, then pour over fresh ice. This smooths the spice and helps it bloom.
