# Zesty Italian Dressing Recipe

This homemade Italian dressing is easy to make, keeps well in the
refrigerator, and uses common pantry ingredients.

## Ingredients

-   1 cup vinegar (apple cider vinegar, wine vinegar, rice vinegar,
    etc.)
-   1-1/3 cups olive oil or other oil
-   2 tablespoons water
-   1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
-   1/2 tablespoon onion powder
-   1/2 tablespoon honey, white sugar, or sucanat
-   1 tablespoon dried oregano
-   1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
-   1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
-   1/2 teaspoon dried basil
-   1/2 tablespoon dried parsley
-   1 tablespoon salt

## Instructions

1.  Add all ingredients to a glass jar or bottle with a tight-fitting
    lid.
2.  If using a narrow-neck bottle, add the dry ingredients first.
3.  Cover and shake thoroughly until well combined.
4.  Use immediately or refrigerate.
5.  Shake before each use.

## Small-Batch Version

Based on the handwritten notes included with the recipe:

-   1/2 cup vinegar
-   1/2 cup olive oil
-   1 tablespoon water
-   3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
-   3/4 teaspoon onion powder
-   3/4 teaspoon honey or sugar
-   1/2 tablespoon oregano
-   1/4 teaspoon pepper
-   1/8 teaspoon thyme
-   1/4 teaspoon basil
-   1/4 tablespoon dried parsley
-   1 teaspoon salt
## Notes

-   The dressing keeps well in the refrigerator for an extended period.
-   Omitting one or two spices will still produce a flavorful dressing.
-   Shake before serving, as natural separation will occur.
